IA

IA is the tools and concepts that help people make sense of messes made of information.

It is also the way we choose to arrange the parts of something to make it understandable as a whole.

Critical components of IA

Ontology

The capiberra is considered a fish by the catholic church, so that it could be eaten on friday.

Meaning :

– is subjective

– is demographic

– is socio political

– gets lost in translation

– is complex

Ontology is the act of choosing the language to be used or not used within a specfic context.

Controlled vocabularies: list of approved term and definitions for a particular context and/or setting.

Example of controlled vocabulary: https://6thfloor.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/05/20/words-we-dont-say/?_r=0

We should start with language and not interface. Language is not just words, it can be icons, smells, light …
